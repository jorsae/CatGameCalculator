How to use Cat Game Calculator

  1. First you want to input what items you want to calculate. This can be done in 2 different ways:
    1. By using pre-made floors, you can add crafting items for whole floors at a time. Or in the case of club events for cat/decoration at a time.
    2. You can scroll down to the desired crafting item and manually type or use the arrow keys to increase/decrease the value.
  2. Select the Crafting time.
    1. This is the time you want to use to craft all your items.
    2. Lower time will mean you will craft fewer items at a time, but it will be cheaper
    3. Higher time will mean you will craft more items at a time, but at a increased cost
  3. These images can be utilised for tips. By clicking on them.
  4. Press the "Calculate meow" button
  5. You can also export the calculations, by clicking the "Copy to clipboard" button and pasting it in excel/google sheet or something similar.

Example Usages

  1. I want to figure out how many materials I need for floor 18
    1. Select floor: 18
    2. Click on the "Quick calculate" button
    3. Done. You can now see all the materials needed for floor 18.
    4. Since you did not change the crafting time, the cost will be inaccurate, as this goes under the assumption that you will craft all items at the same time.
  2. I want to be able to craft 1 Needle every 30 minutes
    1. Manually select 1 needle as crafting input
    2. Set the crafting time to 30minutes
    3. Press the "Calculate meow" button
    4. You can now see in the calculations under "Crafting method" how you need to craft
    5. So in this example, you need to craft:

        String: 1 at a time, 2 times.

        Wood: 1 at a time, 2 times.

        Ribbon: 1 at a time, 1 time.

        Metal: 2 at a time, 2 times.

        Needle: 1 at a time, 1 time.
